# Terre & Tide

## Register

Brand.

## Product purpose

Present Terre & Tide, a fictional Lisbon ceramic lighting studio, through a single-page Signature experience. One sculptural lamp travels through the story, connecting earth, water, fire, and the finished room before arriving beside the enquiry action.

## Audience

- Interior designers sourcing small-batch statement lighting.
- Hospitality and residential architects.
- Private collectors who care about material, provenance, and warm light.

## Primary actions

1. Understand the material and making process.
2. Browse the small collection.
3. Email the studio about a residential or trade commission.

## Brand voice

Sun-warmed, tactile, assured. Plain sensory language with useful specifications.

## Strategic principles

- The object is the narrator.
- Material detail matters more than lifestyle claims.
- Commission lead time and handmade variation are stated clearly.
- Motion supports continuity and pauses for reduced-motion visitors.

## Anti-references

- Beige luxury templates with tiny serif text.
- Coastal clichés and blue-and-white Portuguese motifs.
- Fake editorial endorsements or architect testimonials.

## Content constraints

No fake testimonials or awards. No em dashes in public copy.
