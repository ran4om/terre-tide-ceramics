# Terre & Tide Design System

## Physical scene

Late afternoon sun crosses a Lisbon studio floor, catching mineral dust, wet clay, and the uneven edge of a newly fired shade.

## Direction

Reference lane: a material sample room with the pacing of a gallery installation. A custom SVG lamp is the decisive image and stays present through the narrative. Large fields of clay color replace conventional cards.

## Color strategy

Committed warm palette in OKLCH:

- Chalk: `oklch(96% 0.018 80)`
- Terracotta: `oklch(62% 0.13 42)`
- Glaze: `oklch(45% 0.08 193)`
- Wine: `oklch(31% 0.09 20)`
- Lamp light: `oklch(88% 0.12 82)`

## Typography

Display: Grenze, chosen for broad hand-shaped forms without defaulting to fashion-editorial refinement. Body: Manrope for direct specifications and accessible reading.

## Layout

A sticky object stage occupies one side while chapters move around it. Sections change color and alignment. Collection links behave like a horizontal material shelf, not a card grid.

## Motion

The lamp translates, rotates, scales, and changes glow with scroll progress. Hover states reveal glaze fields through clipping and image zoom. Reduced motion keeps the lamp in a stable sticky position and disables transitions.

## Accessibility

Readable 18px body, clear focus states, semantic headings, no scroll-jacking. The SVG is described once and hidden from screen readers where decorative. Mobile places the object between text chapters.
